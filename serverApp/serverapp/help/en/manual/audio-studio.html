Audio guide creation

Console for generating artwork audios and audio guide introductory tracks.

Step 1. Generate the voice audio

The audio text is taken from the content and the voice must be selected from the voices available in the language ( voice library ).

Note that the generated audio should not exceed 2.30 minutes in length, i.e. approximately 500 words.

Step 2. Add music intro

This step is optional. The music can be selected from the files available in the system ( music library ) or you can paste a URL to the music file for the system to download and use. In this case the user is responsible for having the license to use that music file.

Step 3. Integrate

If the final audio is integrated into the content, it will replace the existing audio of the content — if any — otherwise it will be saved in the audio studio of the content but will not be used in the audio guide.
