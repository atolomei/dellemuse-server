Audio Guide Information

An Audio guide includes an exhibition introductory audio y audios todas o parte de de las artworks and objects in the exhibition. Audio files can be generated and published in all the site's languages.

The audio guide can be of two types: General o Accessible ( icono ) and visitors can choose in the public portal the type of audio guides they access.

Only audio guides in published state are accessible by visitors on the public portal. Those in deleted state will be automatically removed from the system. The icons are:

Edition
Deleted

The audio number is used in the audio number search, it must be added to the physical artwork or object on display so that the visitor can use it when standing in front of the object and wants to listen to the corresponding audio on their phone.
