Archivo de artworks and objects

The site's artwork and object archive belongs to the site. Each one can be added to exhibitions (as an exhibition artwork). When adding an artwork, the artist may not exist in the site's archive, in which case you must go to the Artists section and add it before completing the artwork editing.
