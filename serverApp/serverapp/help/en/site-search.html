Search by audio number

If there is a single audio with the searched audio number (exhibition intro audio o artwork audio), the system directly opens the corresponding page.

Why can there be more than one content with the same audio number?

Los audios de artworks and objects en una audio guía se indexan con el número de audio de la obra u objeto, por lo que si está incluido en más de una audio guía (por ejemplo en la audio guía general of the exhibition and in the accesible of the exhibition) it is possible that there is more than one result for a search.
